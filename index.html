About

I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Sweden. My research focuses on power electronics for renewable-to-hydrogen energy systems, including electrolyzer power supplies, multi-port DC–DC converter topologies, impedance modeling and stability in weak/converter-dominated grids, and reliability-aware control and protection. I am interested in collaborations on hydrogen power conversion and system-level control.
